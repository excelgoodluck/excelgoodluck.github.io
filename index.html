About Me

I'm Excel Goodluck — a creative from Nigeria popularly known as Greatness✌️. I live at the intersection of artistry and technology, crafting visuals that don't just look good but say something.

My work spans graphics design and 3D animation — from brand identities to immersive animated worlds. I believe every pixel is a chance to communicate something powerful.
